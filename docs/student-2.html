DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

Domestic violence is the mistreatment of one family member or intimate partner by another. The abuse can be physical, sexual, verbal, emotional and psychological. Most often (but not always) the persons who abuse are men

WHAT IS DOMESTIC VIOLENCE?

Domestic violence includes any behaviour that is violent, controlling, threatening and intended to make you feel scared and unsafe. Domestic violence can be committed by a family member, spouse of any gender, an ex-partner, a care/guardian, and anyone close or intimate relationship with you

ARE YOU BEING ABUSED?

If you are being abused, you are not alone. There are people and organizations that can help you.

WHAT ARE THE SIGNS
  • They seem afraid of their partner
  • They stopped seeing their friends or family
  • Their children seem afraid of the person
  • They talk about their partners temper or jealousy
  • Their partner often criticises, humiliates, and orders them around
