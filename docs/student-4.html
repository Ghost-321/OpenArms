Help Is Available

Main support service for women affected by sexual and domestic violence:

1800RESPECT 1800 737 723
Emergency (police, ambulance) 000
Full Stop Australia 1800 385 578

What they provide — 1800RESPECT

24/7 confidential counselling, information, online chat, SMS, and referrals for people affected by domestic or sexual violence.

What they provide — Emergency

Call immediately if you or someone else is in immediate danger.

What they provide — Full Stop Australia

Free, confidential counselling, advocacy, and support for people affected by sexual and domestic violence, and their family members and professionals.
